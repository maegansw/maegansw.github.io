Photograph is about finding out what can happen in the frame. When you put four edges around the same facts, you change those facts.

Garry Winogrand was an American street photographer from the Bronx, New York, know for his portrayal of U.S. life and its social issues, in the mis-20th century. Though he photographed in Los Angeles and elsewhere, Winogrand was essentially a New York photographer.
