Why Join TKH?

I think joining The Knowledge House would be a great opportunity for me to fully immerse myself in learning UX/UI Design. The learning model of one year gives me a chance to properly learn the fundamental design principles, create my unique projects, participate in technology events, network with my peers and mentors, and have fun learning. Unlike the other boot camps, the process would not feel rushed. All good things take time, and I feel TKH values this notion. What I find to be invaluable about the TKH learning experience is the ability to have a network of ethnically and culturally similar people to myself. Reading the success stories from previous fellows about how they were able to find jobs and now thriving in their careers, gave me hope that one day I will be like them. As a black woman trying to break into the tech space, it can be difficult to find camaraderie in a white male-dominated field. Having a support system would motive and push me to do my best every day.
