One Small Step is an Oscar nominated animated short produced by TAIKO Studios. It tells the story of a young Chinese-American girl named Luna Chu, who apires to become an astronaut. From the moment she saw her first rocket launch, she knew that's all she ever wanted to do. She faces adversity in pursuit of her dream, but with the support of her loving father, she can get through them.
